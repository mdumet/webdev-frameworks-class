About

Aja is one of 10 concepts from Here to Serve Restaurants, a hospitality company founded by Master Chef Tom Catherall in 1996. Located in the heart of Buckhead at the Alliance Center, the pan-Asian concept’s atmosphere is hip and flirty with a spacious patio overlooking Lenox Road. The menu offers a broad range of freshly prepared dishes inspired from cuisines and ingredients across Asia, including China, Vietnam, Korea and Malaysia, as well as an extensive sushi menu and sushi bar, and crafted cocktails that incorporate enticing Asian ingredients.
